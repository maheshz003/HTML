Mission, Vision & Our Values

Bangalore International Public School

Bangalore International Public School is one of the premier public schools in India, established in 1999, has an imposing history of 25+ years in the field of education, with a vision and mission to impart holistic education to its students.

Our Core Values

It is our school’s endeavour to provide students with opportunities to learn in multidimensional ways and explore the limitless possibilities in their quest for knowledge. Since inception, our overarching goal has been to make BIPS a happy school, where teaching is a pleasure and learning is a joy; where learning and excelling is a passion, culture and values have a pre-eminent place. Our caring and committed teachers are our pillars of strength. They teach our students not just with their minds but with their hearts, making learning enjoyable, rewarding and instilling in students sound values and attributes.

Experience Our Campus Life

Our Vision

To be a world-renowned school, recognized for our distinguished teaching pedagogy, empowering every bipites to emerge as a trailblazer, holding the torch of knowledge high, armed with learning enhanced by national, global and multicultural perspectives.

Our Mission

To provide unparalleled education of the highest quality and an environment of motivation, support and intellectual stimulation to ensure that our students develop the abilities to become front runners in their chosen field and transform into the best version of themselves. Our excellent educational programmes are complemented by state-of-the-art facilities and resources, and our focus on sports and co-curricular opportunities facilitates children’s holistic education.

Academics

Pre-Primary

A pre-school is a cradle that nurtures the young minds to learn and embrace this beautiful world on their own. For it is here that the child takes the first steps, perhaps baby steps towards learning. The time spent at the (Shining Star) Pre primary would thus be termed as one akin to laying the foundation for a child.

ICSE & ISC

The curriculum of ICSE & ISC helps the students to gain total learning experiences of individuals not only in school but in society as well. At BIPS, curriculum development plays a vital role in improving the economy of a country by ensuring that the students are well-versed in all the scholastic and co-scholastic domains required in the ICSE syllabus.

STATE

In order to provide an education most relevant to the age, the academic activity of the school is advised, and pedagogic practices are regulated by a panel of subject experts. The curricular design is based upon principles of wholesomeness in schooling, allowing for a variety of different academic choices in the senior years.

Bangalore International Public School offers holistic development to the students-mentally, physically and spiritually.
